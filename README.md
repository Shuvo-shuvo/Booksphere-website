# 📚 BookSphere – Online Book Marketplace & Community

**BookSphere** is a comprehensive web platform designed for buying, selling, reviewing, and sharing books within a community-driven ecosystem. It aims to connect book lovers across Bangladesh through a unified digital space.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

BookSphere was developed as part of the **CSE 380 coursework** at **Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET)** under the Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering (ETE).

This platform addresses the need for:

* A centralized book marketplace
* Community-based interaction among readers
* Easy book trading and reviewing system
